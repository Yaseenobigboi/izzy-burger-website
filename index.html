THE STORY

Ten Years of Flame-Grilled Excellence

Since 2016, we've been serving Cape Town the juiciest, most flavorful burgers around. Our secret? Real flame grills, quality beef, and no shortcuts. Just honest, delicious food that keeps you coming back.

Quality ingredients are at the heart of everything we do. We source only the freshest produce daily—crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and hand-cut onions. Our beef is never frozen, arriving fresh each morning and flame-grilled to perfection. Every burger is made to order, ensuring maximum freshness from grill to plate. It's this commitment to quality ingredients and absolute freshness that makes every IzzyBurger experience unforgettable.
